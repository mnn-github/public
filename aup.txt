## Purpose
This policy defines acceptable and prohibited use of company-provided internet access and information systems to protect the organization, its users, and its data.

## Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and third parties who access company networks, devices, or cloud services, whether working onsite or remotely.

## Acceptable Use
Users may access the internet for legitimate business purposes and limited personal use, provided that such use:
- Does not interfere with job responsibilities or system performance
- Does not violate company policies, laws, or regulations
- Does not expose the organization to security, legal, or reputational risk

## Prohibited Use
Access to the following types of websites and services is not permitted and may be blocked:
- Malicious or high-risk sites (malware, phishing, command-and-control, anonymizers)
- Illegal content or activities
- Adult, pornographic, or explicit content
- Gambling websites and services
- Peer-to-peer file sharing and unauthorized software downloads
- Sites used to bypass security controls (VPNs, proxies, Tor, DNS tunneling)
- Any websites that violate company policies or applicable laws

## Monitoring and Enforcement
Internet usage may be monitored and logged for security and compliance purposes. Attempts to bypass security controls are prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.

## Exceptions
Business-related exceptions may be granted with documented approval from IT or Security and will be reviewed periodically.

## Violations
Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment, and may involve legal consequences if applicable.

## Acknowledgment
By using company systems and internet access, users acknowledge and agree to comply with this Acceptable Use Policy.
